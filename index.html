Who we are

A campus
staple, worth
finding.

Array Cafe is a beloved neighborhood gathering spot in Urbana where genuine hospitality meets quality craft. This independent café combines thoughtfully prepared coffee with delicious lunch options, creating the kind of welcoming atmosphere you won't find at chain establishments. With cozy seating and a strong community following, Array Cafe invites you to slow down, connect with neighbors, and savor food and coffee made with real care.
